# 🎅GiftGuru🎁
## What is GiftGuru?
GiftGuru is your personalized gift recommendation platform, designed to take the guesswork out of gift-giving. Whether you're shopping for a birthday, anniversary, holiday, or just because, GiftGuru provides tailored suggestions that match the recipient's interests, preferences, and personality.

Explore a curated selection of unique and thoughtful gift ideas from trusted sources, all organized to make your shopping experience effortless and enjoyable. With intelligent search features, a user-friendly interface, and expert recommendations, GiftGuru ensures you always find the perfect gift for every occasion.
